Retro Snake Game

Instructions: Use your arrow/WASD keys to move the yellow block and "eat" the red block. The snake will grow in length the more blocks it eats. If the snake touches a wall or itself it's game over!

Login/register on the sidebar in the right to chat with other players. The highest scores are displayed below. You can always pause the game by clicking your spacebar.

Controls

  1. W or Up Arrow - Up
  2. A or Left Arrow - Left
  3. S or Down Arrow - Down
  4. D or Right Arrow - Right
